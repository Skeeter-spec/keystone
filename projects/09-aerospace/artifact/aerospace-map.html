Eight firms that cannot be routed around: two large jetliner makers (Boeing and Airbus form a duopoly), three jet engine designers (GE Aerospace, Rolls-Royce, and RTX form an oligopoly), one aerospace titanium supplier (VSMPO-Avisma supplies roughly a third of global aerospace titanium), and two structural castings makers (Precision Castparts and Howmet Aerospace). All eight are either duopoly/oligopoly players or single-source suppliers for mission critical components, with lead times measured in years that prevent quick supplier switching. Every call here is a foundation hypothesis until relationship edges test it.
